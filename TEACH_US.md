## Shift-Left Observability

A practice I believe could improve engineering productivity is "Shift-Left Observability."

Traditionally, observability is introduced after deployment through dashboards, logs and monitoring systems.

Instead, teams can make observability part of the development workflow from day one.

Every feature should be developed alongside:

* Structured logging
* Health checks
* Metrics
* Error tracking
* Traceability

For example, when building a new API endpoint, engineers should think about:

* What should be logged?
* How will failures be detected?
* What metrics should be measured?
* How will support teams debug issues?

This approach reduces production incidents and shortens debugging time because visibility is designed into the system rather than added later.

As systems grow, observability becomes a competitive advantage because teams can move faster with greater confidence.
